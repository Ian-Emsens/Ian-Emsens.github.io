Who am I

Cat Person, born in '95 and still kickin'. Impulsive road trip planner that spends too much time awake.

Gaming since the Pentium II era and graduated in '16 with great pleasure and distinction at Thomas More Mechelen's Interactive Multimedia & Design.

I create things, online

I enjoy making things, working on that special animation or fine-tuning that little bit of responsive motion.

And when all the little things click, when all the little cogs have been oiled and my creation reaches its final form, I'll feel a huge rush of excitement.

That rush and the feeling of accomplishment that goes with it is why I love what I do.

Voting App Logo, by Antwerp

Antwerp Voting App

Antwerp was looking for a way to modernise and improve their voting infrastructure.

To help them achieve this goal, I spearheaded a project at Flow Pilots to create a multi-client redux-based Angular application with live update functionality through websockets.

I'd love to show you but the actual application itself is locked in a carefully secured environment to avoid any political tampering. Which was also a key cornerstone during development.

Project Jackhammer

As a creative outlet, I started up a little side project while I was in college. It aimed to draw Smite's characters in a very minimalist 'flat' style.

It gained a little bit of traction and eventually got taken over by an affiliate of Hi-Rez Studios, the creators of Smite, to be used in their clothing line.
